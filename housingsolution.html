Boom Barrier

Societies today want to secure their premises absolutely and having boom barriers and RFID (Radio Frequency Identification) vehicle tags allows them to keep track of vehicles and visitors entering and leaving the building.

With boom barriers in place, only vehicles that have RFID tags provided by the society can enter the building and have access to the gate. Vehicles that do not have RFID tags will be denied entry, at least, until they have authorised approval.
